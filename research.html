Research Focus:

UAV (Unmanned Aerial Vehicle) or Drone Technology:
Secure Communications in UAV, Authentication Protocols, Long-range communication for drones.

Fog/Edge Computing:
-Edge Intelligence
-Computation offloading in resource-constrained devices and verifiable computation

Long-range and scalable IoT networks using WiFi HaLow.
My research at C2PS focused on the design and implementation of lightweight and context-aware authentication protocols for drone Remote ID, addressing critical security challenges in compliance with ASTM standards for UAV airspace. I am also working on context-aware drone Remote ID authentication and optimized observer placements for securing the airspace for drones. For more details about our projects please visit Secure UTM Systems Lab.
